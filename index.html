Design

Design is the process of envisioning and creating solutions that are both aesthetically pleasing and functional. It involves a thoughtful and deliberate approach to problem-solving, where ideas are translated into tangible forms or experiences.

New Media

New media refers to the use of emerging technologies and digital platforms to create, distribute, and interact with artistic and creative content. It encompasses a wide range of mediums, including digital art, virtual reality, augmented reality, interactive installations, video games, web-based projects, and social media.

Performing Arts

Performing arts encompass a range of artistic disciplines that are performed live for an audience. It includes forms such as theater, dance, music, opera, and spoken word. Performing arts involve the use of the human body, voice, and various artistic techniques to convey stories, emotions, and ideas.

Visual Arts

Visual arts encompass various forms of creative expression that primarily rely on visual elements such as color, shape, line, texture, and composition. It includes disciplines such as painting, drawing, sculpture, photography, printmaking, and mixed media.
